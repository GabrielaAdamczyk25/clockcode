'Untitled' (Perfect Lovers)

Felix Gonzalez-Torres, 1987–1990

In the original artwork two identical, battery-powered clocks are set to the same time but eventually go out of sync. Made shortly after the passing of the author's partner, they're designed to track the inevitable flow of time and portray the fragility of human relationships.

The Infinite Scroll

Aza Raskin, 2006

An interface element in which the user triggers instantaneous loading of content by scrolling down an internet page. Creating a seemingly endless stream of content, the mechanism prompts users to engage in unwillingly long sessions of scrolling through their social media feed, catching them in a loop of addiction.

'The Persistence of Memory'

Salvador Dalí, 1931

Inspired by Dalí's observation of camembert cheese melting in the sun, here the clocks succumb to the logic of dreaming by melting and distorting, thus playing on the relativity of time.
